Experiential Learning

Learning by doing is one of the cornerstones of my classroom. My students tend to walk into my classroom with a lot of theory but not very much practice. I use experiential learning as a way to help my students connect what they know with what they do and, ultimately, I hope that it helps them learn more deeply.

Reflection

Reflection is a technique that is often associated with disciplines that are writing based, such as English, but it can be used as an effective tool in any discipline. I used refelction in my classroom to encourage my students to evaluate their own learning and experiences on a deeper level.

About

This research is the culminating project in the Penn Master's Residency in Teaching Program. This program takes an innovative approach to teacher training by deeply intertwining theory and practice.

Student Work

Student work is at the center of this research. The students in robotics have created some amazing projects. They are currently working on their final projects which include a drone that follows a user around, a hockey puck launcher, and a robotic hand!
