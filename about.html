Penn Master's Residency in Teaching Program

The Penn Master's Residency in Teaching (PRMT) Program is a specialized teacher education program for early career boarding school teachers. Cohort members are employed by one of the nine participating boarding schools and meet with their cohort four times a year in person. The rest of the coursework is done online. The program blends theory and practice in an innovative way in an attempt to prepare the next generation of outstanding teachers. The program will soon be expanding to include a day school cohort.

I designed and coded this website from scratch for this inquiry project.
